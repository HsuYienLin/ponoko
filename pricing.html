What is manufacturer pricing?

Our ‘manufacturer pricing' is for businesses who want lowest possible cost. It's designed to ensure you profit when selling your products at both wholesale (2x manufacturer pricing) and retail (4x manufacturer pricing). Manufacturer pricing is less for small & simple parts than it is for big & complex ones.
